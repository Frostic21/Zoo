Bottlenose Dolphin

Dolphins are one of the people's favourite sea animals. They are adorable and are very playful creatures. They can form friendships that can lst decades in which they hunt or play together.

Dolphins grow to be anywhere from 6 to 12 feet long. They shed their outermost layer of skin every two hours.

Dolphins diet consists of fish, squid, and krill. They have a special skill in which they can use echolocation. They also have extremely good hearing which they can use to hear other dolphins.
